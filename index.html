FAQ

Questions I'd ask too

Does it require an internet connection?

No — once VoxFlow is installed and the recognition model is downloaded on first launch (about 140MB), you do not need the internet to use it. Transcription runs entirely on your machine. The only exception is updates: when a new version is released, VoxFlow will notify you and you'll need an internet connection to download it.

How accurate is it compared to cloud-based tools?

Very accurate. It handles natural speech, accents, and technical language well. For most everyday use you won't notice a meaningful difference from cloud-based alternatives.

Will it work in the apps I actually use?

Yes. VoxFlow injects text at the cursor position using your keyboard, which means it works in anything — browsers, Word, Slack, VS Code, games, whatever you have open.

What does my PC need to run it?

Windows 10 or 11, 64-bit, and a microphone. At least 4GB of RAM is recommended for smooth performance. No GPU required.

What happens when you release updates?

VoxFlow checks for updates automatically in the background. When one is available a notification appears in the pill widget and in Settings. Nothing installs without your say so.

Something broke. How do I reach you?

There's a bug reporter built into the app under Settings → About. It opens an email with your system info pre-filled. You can also email [email protected] directly. I read everything.
